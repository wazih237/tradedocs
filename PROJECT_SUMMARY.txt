TEMPLATE DETAILS:
==============================================================================

Each template includes:
- id (slug): Unique identifier
- name: Display name
- category: Category ID reference
- description: Specific to commodity trading with regulatory references
- price: USD pricing (range: $35-$499)
- commodities: Array of commodity types (Grains, Metals, Oils, etc.)
- markets: Geographic markets (Global, China Mainland, etc.)
- status: "mandatory" | "recommended" | "optional"
- popularity: Score 1-100 (based on trading importance)

REGULATORY REFERENCES INCLUDED:
- GAFTA: Grain and Feed Trade Association contracts
- FOSFA: Federation of Oils, Seeds and Fats Associations
- UCP 600: Uniform Customs and Practice for Documentary Credits
- GACC: General Administration of Customs (China)
- GB 7718: Chinese food labeling standard
- ISO/ASTM: International quality standards
- FTA: Free Trade Agreements (CPTPP, RCEP)
- FSSC 22000: Food Safety Management
- OFAC/UN/EU: Sanctions compliance
- Basel & Rotterdam Conventions: Hazardous materials
